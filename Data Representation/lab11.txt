Contrary to Lab 10, Lab 11 uses named constants for the addends instead of immediate operands. Four doublewords are used in the data section to represent the addends, and only the eax register is used. The contents of all registers are shown after each step in the calculation, and the program adheres to the ordinary rules of algebraic precedence and associativity. The program compiles with the Makefile when a target of lab11 is added to targetsAsmLanguage.
