Lab 8 is a pushdown transducer that accepts the set consisting of:

{a^i * b^i, c^k | i, j > 0 and k = min(i,j)}.

Valid strings:
ab,c;
aab,c;
abb,c;
aaab,c;
aabb,cc;
abbb,c;
aaaab,c;
aaabb,cc;
aabbb,cc;

Invalid strings:
elem,elem;
a,elem;
b,elem;
aa,elem;
ba,elem;
bb,elem;
aba,c;
baa,elem;
bab,elem;
bba,elem;
bbb,elem;
